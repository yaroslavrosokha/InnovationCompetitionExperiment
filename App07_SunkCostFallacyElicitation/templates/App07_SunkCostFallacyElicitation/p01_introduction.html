For Task #19, you are the sole entrepreneur in the market facing a two-period investment problem. Specifically, in the first period, you are endowed with $15 and have to initiate a research project at a cost of $5 (leaving you with $10). You know that there is uncertainty about the cost to complete the project in the second period. Specifically, the costs to complete the project takes one of the values {0.5,1,...,10}. A completed project has a value of $7.5, and an uncompleted project has a value of $0. In the second period you will observe the completion cost of the project and then choose between two options -- Option A and Option B:

  • Option A: Complete the project at the completion cost and receive profit of [Endowment - $5]+[$7.5 - Project Completion Cost]
  • Option B: Do not complete the project and receive profit of [Endowment - $5]

Next, you will initiate the project at a cost of $5. After that you will make 20 decisions (one for each of the realized completion cost values) using the decision table similar the previous tasks.
